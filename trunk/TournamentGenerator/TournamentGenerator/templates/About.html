About


Who are we?

We are Alexander Büchel, Fabio Hilti and Lucy Gannon and we are the creator of the Tournament Generator, which we have created as our Information Systems Development Group Project.

To be compete with one and each other is in the human nature – it is fun and challenging. All of us three in our group like to participate in different competitions, respectively tournaments in a sport club or just for fun with friends. With that thought in our mind, we developed the idea to create a “Tournament Generator”.
