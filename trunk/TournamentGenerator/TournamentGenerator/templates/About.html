About


Who are we?

We are Alexander Büchel, Fabio Hilti and Lucy Gannon and we are the creators of the Tournament Generator, which we have created for our Information Systems Development Group Project.

To be compete with others is part of human nature – it is fun and challenging. All of us in our group like to participate in different competitions and tournaments for sports or just for fun with friends. With that thought in our mind, we developed the idea to create a “Tournament Generator”.
